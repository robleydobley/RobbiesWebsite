I am currently trying to set up the navigation bar. This consists of links for Resume, Cover Letter and Career Goals.
I wanted the links to stack upon one another so I embedded them in <p>. This worked to stack them but it has disabled the links.
Currently researching to correct.

Solution 1.
I was mistakenly replaced the <a anchor with p. (See top two lines). I resolved the issue by embedding the <a wihin <p>.
    <p href="coverLetter.html">Cover Letter</p>
    <p href="careerGoals.html">Career Goals</p>

    <p><a href="index.html">Home</a></p>
    <p><a href="resume.html">Resume</a></p>
